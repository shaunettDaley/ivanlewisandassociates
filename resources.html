    What Is a Tax Credit?

    Subtract tax credits from the amount of tax you owe. There are two types of tax credits:

    • gradeA nonrefundable tax credit means you get a refund only up to the amount you owe.
    • gradeA refundable tax credit means you get a refund, even if it's more than what you owe.

    Subtract tax deductions from your income before you figure the amount of tax you owe.
